## 4. Shot List

```markdown
| Shot | Time | Frame | Camera | Blocking | Action | Sound | Subtitle | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | 0-3s | CU/Wide/etc | static/push/track |  |  |  |  | hook |
```

Rules:

- Shot 1 must contain conflict.
- Every 10-15 seconds needs escalation.
- Use at least 3 object close-ups in a 60-90s episode.
- Include one silence beat before the main reveal.
