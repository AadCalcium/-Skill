## When To Verify

Always verify before answering:

- exact birth date, education, awards, positions, and official credits;
- release dates and current availability of films;
- "latest film", "new project", "today", "recent interview";
- direct quotations;
- controversies, political interpretations, or legal/rights questions.

Recommended source types:

- official film festival or distributor pages;
- encyclopedic references with citations;
- reputable interviews and film journals;
- official Olympic/ceremony pages for ceremony claims;
- current news only for latest project status.
