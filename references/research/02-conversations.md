# 02 · 公开创作谈抽象出的工作原则

Use this file when the user wants a "director's thinking process", not only a finished script. Do not present these as verbatim quotes unless independently verified from a reliable source.

## Principle 1：先有可以被记住的画面

Operational meaning:

- Before writing dialogue, find the visual contradiction that summarizes the story.
- A good short-drama scene should still make sense as a silent contact sheet.
- If the contact sheet looks like generic talking heads, the director system has failed.

Prompts to ask:

- What image would make the viewer stop scrolling?
- What object in the frame contains the secret?
- What color or shape tells us who has power?

## Principle 2：把文化符号变成动作，不变成装饰

Operational meaning:

- Ritual is useful only when someone must obey or break it.
- Folk texture is useful only when it shapes choices.
- Traditional objects are useful only when they carry risk.

Bad:

- Lanterns because "Chinese style".
- Drums because "epic".
- Red clothes because "Zhang Yimou".

Good:

- Lanterns mark whose turn it is to be judged.
- Drumbeat stops when the lie is exposed.
- Red cloth hides a name, a wound, or a debt record.

## Principle 3：让制度和人同时进入镜头

Operational meaning:

- Do not write an abstract "society is unfair" line.
- Put the person at a window, counter, desk, gate, table, queue, doorway, or ranking board.
- Let the audience see the system as spatial arrangement.

Short-drama examples:

- Hospital cashier glass between daughter and receipt.
- School honor board behind a bullied child.
- Company morning meeting line swallowing the whistleblower.
- Family banquet seating chart trapping the bride.

## Principle 4：群众不是背景，是压力装置

Operational meaning:

- Crowd direction matters more than crowd size.
- A few bodies can become a wall, circle, corridor, staircase, or witness stand.
- Reactions should be choreographed: silence, simultaneous turning, one person sitting down first, phones rising together.

## Principle 5：现实主义要有手感

Operational meaning:

- The surface of things matters: wet sleeve, chipped bowl, thermal cup stain, old file string, greasy table, cracked loudspeaker.
- Short-drama AI prompts should include tactile details only when they carry story.
- Do not over-polish poor spaces into luxury visuals.

## Principle 6：反转要有道德后果

Operational meaning:

- A reveal is weak if it only surprises.
- A reveal is strong if it forces the crowd to revise who is guilty, who benefited, and who paid the price.
- The object that proves the truth should have appeared earlier.

## Director's internal checklist

Before generating:

1. Can I describe the episode with one image?
2. Does every ritual have a breaker?
3. Does every object have an arc?
4. Does every crowd pattern pressure a specific person?
5. Does the final reveal change moral responsibility?
